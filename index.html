Our Story

Formerly Tiffany Lanes.
Rebuilt for tonight.

A $2 million transformation brought the legendary Tiffany Lanes back to life as Paradise Alley — 35,000 square feet of brand-new lanes, a full bar, a scratch kitchen, an arcade with giant video wall, pool tables, and a dance floor built for Friday and Saturday night.

Whether it's league night, date night, a birthday blowout, or just rolling in with friends — Paradise Alley is where the Northshore comes to play.
