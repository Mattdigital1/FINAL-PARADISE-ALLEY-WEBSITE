Good to Know

Lane Policies

No Reservations. All lanes are walk-in only. During busy periods, your name goes on the waitlist at the front desk and we'll call you when a lane opens up.
Proper Shoes Required. Street shoes are not allowed on the lanes. Rental shoes are available for $4/person at the front desk.
Food & Drink at the Lanes. You're welcome to order from the bar and kitchen while you bowl — our staff will bring it right to your lane.
Minimum Age. All ages welcome during regular hours. After 9pm on Fridays and Saturdays, guests must be 21+ with valid ID.
