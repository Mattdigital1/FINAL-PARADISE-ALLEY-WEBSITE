Bowling
Leagues

Compete, connect, and keep score all season. Paradise Alley runs adult, youth, and competitive leagues across 32 modern lanes — six days a week.

Why League Bowl?

More Than
Just Bowling

League bowling isn't just about your average. It's the weekly ritual — your crew, your lanes, a cold drink in hand, and the satisfaction of a strike that the whole team hears.

Paradise Alley provides state-of-the-art lanes, automatic scoring, pro shop support, and a full bar to keep things going long after the last frame.

Join a League
What We Offer

Find Your League

Whether you're brand new to bowling or a seasoned competitor, there's a league for you.

Adult Leagues
Men's, women's, and mixed leagues for bowlers 18+. Weekly sessions scheduled across multiple days and times to fit your life.
Youth Leagues
Saturday morning leagues for kids and teens. Learn fundamentals, earn prizes, and build friendships in a fun, supportive environment.
Competitive Leagues
Serious about your game? Our competitive leagues feature handicap systems, season standings, and end-of-season tournaments.
6 Days a Week
League slots available Tuesday through Sunday. Morning, afternoon, and evening options across all 32 lanes.
Pro Shop
On-site pro shop with ball drilling, equipment rentals, and advice from experienced staff. Get fitted right.
Full Season Play
Structured seasons with weekly standings, stat tracking, and year-end awards. Every bowler earns their place on the board.
Getting Started

How to Join

01

Email or Call Us

Reach out to our league coordinator to ask about available leagues, schedules, and openings. We'll match you to the right division for your skill level and availability.

02

Register Your Team

Fill out your team registration form and pay the season fee. Teams are typically 4–5 bowlers. Solo? We'll find you a team or you can bowl as a sub.

03

Show Up & Bowl

Your first night, arrive 15 minutes early to get set up. Lanes, scoring, and your season handbook will be ready. All you need to bring is your A-game.
